Prevent Heartbleed: upgrade openssl with brew on your mac

The Heartbleed Bug is a serious vulnerability in the popular OpenSSL cryptographic software library. Follow brew command will upgrade your openssl library:

brew install openssl && brew link openssl --force Relaunch your Terminal (has to be hard quit ⌘ + Q) and check your openssl version: openssl version Now you shall have OpenSSL 1.0.1g 7 Apr 2014 or newer. #brew #mac #openssl #heartbleed
